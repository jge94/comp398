News

On Sunday, September 21, members of the Wheaton College Equestrian Team took part in a day of community service at BINA Farm Center in Norfolk, MA. BINA is a center that interacts with those with and without special needs through various programs and activities. Their focus is on various horse-related therapy programs, including therapeutic horseback riding and hippotherapy, as well as programs that reach out to the wider community. On Sunday the team helped with BINA's clean-up program to prepare their new barn facilities. Using dusters, brooms, pine-sol, and a heavy dose of elbow grease, team members polished, swept and cobwebbed until the barn shone. The team is grateful for the opportunity to reach out to the community, and hopes to continue their new relationship with BINA.
